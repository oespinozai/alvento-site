01 — Plugin
RegalMail

Email as a first-class channel for AI agents

RegalMail gives your AI agent a full email channel—inbound monitoring, outbound replies, multi-account routing, and conversation threading. Not a wrapper around an API. A complete email system that runs autonomously.

Built for agents that need to read, understand, and reply to real email from real people. Handles the hard parts: IMAP IDLE for real-time delivery, anti-loop protection so agents don't reply to themselves, and threaded replies that land in the right conversation.

What's included

›

Real-time IMAP monitoring — IDLE-based, not polling. Emails arrive in seconds, not minutes.

›

Threaded replies — In-Reply-To and References headers. Replies land in the right conversation thread.

›

Multi-account routing — Route sales@, support@, hello@ to different agents automatically.

›

Anti-loop protection — Detects noreply addresses, auto-reply headers, rate limits. Agents won't loop.

›

Connection resilience — Automatic reconnect with exponential backoff. Survives network drops.

›

Framework agnostic — Works with any agent framework. TypeScript, clean interfaces, no lock-in.
