06 — Questions

FAQ

What is Answer Engine Optimisation?

AEO makes your business the answer when people ask AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity or Google AI a question. Instead of ranking on a page of links, you get cited directly in the AI’s response.

How is this different from SEO?

SEO gets you ranked in search results. AEO gets you cited in AI answers. Different signals matter—entity clarity, structured answers, and source authority rather than just keywords and backlinks. Both work together.

How long until I see results?

Google AI Overviews can pick up changes within weeks. ChatGPT and Perplexity depend on their data refresh cycles. Most clients see measurable improvement within 60–90 days.

Do I still need traditional SEO?

Yes. Strong SEO makes your content more authoritative, which helps AI models trust and cite you. We recommend both. If you need SEO as well, we offer that separately.

Can you guarantee AI citations?

No—same as no one can guarantee a #1 Google ranking. What we guarantee is that your content will be structured and optimised for the signals AI models use to choose sources. The rest is maths: better signals, more citations.

What industries does this work for?

Any business people search for—service firms, professional practices, SaaS, e-commerce, regulated businesses. If customers ask AI “who’s the best X” or “what’s the best solution for Y,” AEO works for you. Clients often add it alongside Alvento’s Systems or Service capability work.

What do I get for £49?
